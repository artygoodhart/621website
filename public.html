Who are we?

You've done your GCSEs. You've worked hard. You're ambitious. You want to make a difference to the world now and not just in the future. You want to go to a good university but you have an eye on your career as well. You want a future filled with spark and excitement and the chance to grow in a variety of ways.

Six21 might be what you're looking for. We have fantastic teachers with experience of getting students into top universities. We also have extraordinary partners who work with us to give you life-changing experiences. Experiences you can't get anywhere else. A career in medicine or science? A career in the creative industries? The chance to work with top creative talents in theatres, film production companies, high tech businesses.
Starting in September 2017, Six21 will be a pioneering new institution for the ambitious, the academic, and the enterprising.
Our aim is to give people the tools to make a difference to the world. Harnessing School 21's track record for developing exceptional students who are eloquent speakers, skilled problem solvers and creative thinkers, Six21 will develop not just great academic qualifications but curious, critical thinkers who innovate with ease and who move with agility from one way of working to another. Our students will leave us ready to thrive in an increasingly complex and uncertain world - they will be masters of their own destiny.

The Six21 Diploma

At Six21 we believe that authentic learning and development only occurs when we challenge all of ourselves. Consequently, we have created the Six21 Diploma to ensure that you experence deep and connected learning:

A-LEVELS & OTHER QUALIFICATIONS
Our courses are taught through a cutting-edge combination of lectures, seminars, oracy-rich discussions and guided study. This way our students learn to be independent and self-motivated thinkers who are ready for the demands of university and the working world.

EXTENDED PROJECT QUALIFICATION (EPQ)
All students will complete the EPQ, which is highly respected by universities and gives you the chance to work independently on a topic you are passionate about (and it's worth half an A-level)

COMMUNITY SERVICE
Our students develop empathy and the skills to make a positive difference in the world through ongoing social action in the community. All students will get the chance to lead a project or campaign that changes the world.

WELLBING CIRRICULUM
All students and all teachers at Six21 are trained as coaches. Our coaching programme focuses on wellbeing and leadership development, helping our students to nd their spark and to use it to make a difference in the world

WORK PLACEMENTS
Six21 has an impressive range of partnerships that students will draw upon to secure their work placements. Off-site for up to one day per week, all of our students will apply their learning in the real world by taking on challenging projects that deliver real results.

PROJECT BASED LEARNING
Our students develop deep understanding of the curriculum's most important ideas by creating work of real beauty and depth.

Pioneering taching

School 21 is already world famous for its high-quality and cutting edge teaching with articles appearing in multiple media outlets including the BBC, Guardian and the Financial Times. Teachers and school leaders have visited from as far away as [insert place here] to see our teaching in practice.

At Six21, we will build on that legacy, incorporating the best pedagogy from schools and universities internationally. You will have a mixture of university-style lectures, including from cutting-edge experts outside the school, seminars, small group and 1:1 sessions. You will participate in Harkness debates, in genuine research and in socratic dialogue as you engage with your sixth form studies on the deepest possible level.

Real world learning

All of your learning at Six21 will be designed around your needs and tailored to your individual aspirations. This will include learning beyond the classroom such as collaborating in research with a university, ongoing and meaningful medical work expereince and completing genuine projects for partner organisations. Through these experiences you will have the opportunities to begin creating your own personal networks, learn how to create and sustain long-lasting change and develop your leaership and teamworking abilities.

Make a difference

At Six21, we believe that all of us have a responsibility to make a positive difference to the world around us. This lies at the heart of our values: humanity includes seeing where we can help others or improve the world; integrity means that we cannot sit back and ignore those issues we have identi ed; striving is the effort that allows us to not just think about problems but actively do something about them.

We believe that you have the responability and capability to contribute to our local and global society in a meaningful and lasting way and this service forms an integral part of our Six21 Diploma. During your time with us you will have the opportunity to create, implement and evaluate a project that will make a difference and begin a life-long commitment to making our world a better place.
